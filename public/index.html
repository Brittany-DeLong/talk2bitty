About Me

I chose to create this space to push myself outside my comfort zone, and maybe, over time, meet some new people. Mental health and human development are things I genuinely enjoy and take incredibly seriously—it's exactly what I'm going to school for. This path matters to me deeply, especially after recently losing someone close to me due to unresolved traumas and mental health struggles. It's something I am still actively trying to process myself.

I saw firsthand what trauma did to them because it was left unprocessed. Even while sitting in professional counseling, they still couldn't open up for whatever reason—maybe because they just didn't feel comfortable enough in that rigid environment. Keeping things locked inside has a devastating outcome. Sometimes people don't need a clinical clipboard watching them; they just need an actual, trustworthy human to talk to.

"I am completely non-judgmental. This is a strict judgment-free zone. I don't judge—I just watch, and people show you their directions over time, you know? That's all. I'm just here to give advice if that's what you want, or simply listen if you just need to vent and talk through whatever you're dealing with. Whether you need to unpack some heavy stuff, or honestly, just bullshit and talk about completely random nonsense because you don't want to feel isolated—you can do that here. If it's hard for you to trust, there is a completely anonymous way to use this space so you can protect your peace while you feel things out."

Honestly, I'm not going to lie about needing the extra income either—look at the gas prices and the cost of living nowadays, it’s ridiculous. I'm a single mom, a student, and I work, so the extra money helps. Because I’m balancing my kids, my schoolwork, and my job, phone calls just aren't feasible for my life. Plus, I can be super shy, straightforward, or very blunt depending on the day. Texting just makes the most sense for all of us to protect our time and space.

⚠️ Important Notice & Boundaries

Text-Based Peer Support Notice: This platform is strictly an informal, text-based peer support space designed for personal venting, sharing life perspectives, and talking through things. While I am currently a college student studying human development and family dynamics, I am not a licensed counselor, therapist, or medical mental health professional. The text conversations here are just person-to-person talks. They are not a substitute for professional clinical therapy or medical treatment. If you are experiencing a severe mental health crisis, an emergency, or having thoughts of self-harm, please exit this page immediately and call a certified professional emergency hotline or dial 988.

Reality & Boundaries

Let's be completely real: I am not going to magically decode your entire life or recognize every pattern in a single text conversation. That's just not realistic. But here is who I am and how I operate:

👁️ My Radar (What I Do)

  • Spot the Loops: If you are stuck in a repeating relationship mess, I excel at zooming out to spot the behavioral patterns you might be blind to.
  • Read Intentions: Hard childhood experiences taught me how to read whether people mean well or mean harm. It's a blessing and a curse, but I'll give it to you straight.
  • Deep Reflector: I break down processing loops cleanly, providing space for raw honesty.
  • Listen Securely: If you need to heavy-dump 30 minutes of complete nonsense or deep pain without an awkward phone call, my text window is a secure vault.

🚫 My Limits (What I Don't Do)

  • No Faked Knowledge: I don't know everything about everything. If you bring me something outside my wheelhouse, I will tell you flat out.
  • Not Professional Therapy: I am a student, but I am NOT a licensed professional right now. This is a peer support space. No crisis therapy.
  • No Inappropriate BS: Treat me with the exact respect you want back. Bring disrespect or explicit nonsense, and the text session ends immediately. Period.
